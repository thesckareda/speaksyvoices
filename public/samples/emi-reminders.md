Bahut badhiya. Payment ke baad aapko SMS confirmation mil jayega. Kya main aapki madad kuch aur kar sakti hoon?

User:
Nahi, bas itna hi. Thank you Priya.

Agent:
Aapka din shubh ho. Speaksy Finance ke saath banking ke liye dhanyavaad!
